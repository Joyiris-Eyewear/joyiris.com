# Joyiris Eyewear Company Profile

---

## I. Company Overview

Joyiris is affiliated with **Shanghai Joyiris Supply Chain Co., LTD.**, headquartered in Shanghai, China, with over 20 years of experience in the eyewear industry. The company specializes in the **R&D, manufacturing integration, and global supply chain management of eyewear products**.

As a **vertically integrated eyewear supply chain company**, Joyiris deeply integrates resources from China’s major eyewear industrial clusters and maintains long-term strategic cooperation with key production bases, including:

- **Wenzhou, Zhejiang**: optical frames and mid-to-high-end acetate frames  
- **Danyang, Jiangsu**: optical lenses, resin lenses, and functional lenses  
- **Xiamen, Fujian**: sunglasses, sports eyewear, and fashion sunglasses  
- **Shenzhen, Guangdong**: high-end metal frames and titanium precision manufacturing  

Through this multi-regional industrial synergy system, Joyiris achieves efficient resource allocation and manufacturing optimization, providing global customers with a stable, diversified, and efficient one-stop eyewear sourcing solution.

Today, Joyiris products are exported to more than **60 countries and regions worldwide**, serving eyewear brands, wholesalers, retailers, and cross-border e-commerce businesses.

## III. Product & Material System

Joyiris covers optical frames, sunglasses, and functional eyewear, serving a wide range of use cases from classic business styles to fashion and sports applications.

Key material systems include:

- **Acetate**: premium texture, rich colors, durable, widely used in mid-to-high-end optical frames  
- **Metal**: lightweight and refined, suitable for business and fashion designs  
- **TR-90**: ultra-light and flexible material with strong impact resistance, ideal for sports and daily wear  

The product line is further extended to functional eyewear, including blue light blocking glasses, sports sunglasses, clip-on sunglasses, and protective goggles.

---

## IV. Quality Control & Global Delivery

Joyiris operates a comprehensive quality management system covering raw material selection, production monitoring, and final product inspection to ensure consistent quality and reliability.

Quality assurance includes:

- Raw material screening and performance testing  
- In-process production inspection and standardization control  
- Final product testing (durability, structural strength, UV protection, etc.)  

Our products comply with **CE certification (EU standards)** and **FDA-related quality requirements**, and undergo multiple international-standard tests such as UV400 protection, fatigue resistance, and salt spray testing.

For global logistics, Joyiris works with leading international carriers including **DHL, FedEx, and UPS**, ensuring efficient and reliable worldwide delivery.
